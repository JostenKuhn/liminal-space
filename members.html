The Field Manual

The complete written reference. Every technique, every step, every detail. Tap any section to expand.

1. Prepare Your Environment

  • Quiet, dark room. 30 minutes uninterrupted.
  • Phone on airplane mode.
  • Stereo headphones (not speakers).
  • Lie on your back. Light blanket. Remove glasses/watch.

2. The Preparation Ritual

  • Conversion Box: Visualize a large, strong container. Place every thought, worry, plan, and fear inside. Close the lid. Everything will be there when you return.
  • Resonant Tuning: Three deep breaths. On each exhale, hum a long, low tone. You are tuning yourself like an instrument.
  • Affirmation: "I am more than my physical body. I am more than what I can see and touch and hear. I am ready to enter the Liminal Space."

3. During the Session

  • Press play on the Door 10 track. Close your eyes.
  • Do not try to force anything. Passive observation only.
  • Notice physical sensations: tingling, heaviness, warmth, vibration.
  • If you see colors or patterns, observe without grasping.

4. After the Session

  • Open eyes slowly. Don't move immediately.
  • Write down everything you experienced, no matter how small.
  • Day 1–2: Door 10 — Getting comfortable with the state
  • Day 3–4: Door 10 — Deepening. Notice the exact moment your body falls asleep while your mind stays awake.
  • Day 5: Door 12 — The remote viewing session. Follow the technique: pick a location, shift from imagining to perceiving.
  • Day 6: Door 12 — Repeat. Notice which details arrive on their own — those are real perception, not imagination.
  • Day 7: Door 15 — The separation session. Try both techniques: the Rope and the Roll-Out. Use whichever feels natural.

Practice at the same time each day. Consistency trains your brain to enter these states faster.

What You Might Experience

  • Deep relaxation — Nearly universal. This alone is valuable.
  • Hypnagogic imagery — Flashes of light, geometric patterns, brief dreamlike scenes.
  • Vibrations — Buzzing or humming throughout the body. This is significant.
  • Floating or sinking — Body feels lighter, heavier, or gently rocking.
  • Time distortion — 20 minutes feels like 5, or like an hour.
  • Sleep — Completely normal. Gets better with practice.

If You Keep Falling Asleep

Try sitting at a slight incline. Practice when alert, not tired. Morning sessions work well.

If You Feel Vibrations But Get Scared

This is the vibrational stage — you're on the right path. Remain calm. Let the vibrations intensify without resistance.
