Overview

Liminal Space ("we", "us", "our") operates the website enterliminalspace.com. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. We keep things simple because we believe your data should be respected, not exploited.

Information We Collect

We collect only what you voluntarily provide:

  • First name and email address when you opt in to receive our free audio track or other content

We do not require account creation. We do not collect passwords, payment card details, or sensitive personal data on this site.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for:

  • Delivering the free audio track and PDF guide you requested
  • Sending email communications about Liminal Space, including product updates, new releases, and educational content

Third-Party Services

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to operate:

  • Beehiiv — email marketing platform that stores your name and email address and delivers our emails
  • Stan Store — payment processor for product purchases. Stan Store handles all payment data directly; we never see or store your card details
  • Google Analytics (GA4) — anonymous website analytics to understand how visitors use our site. No personally identifiable information is shared
  • Meta Pixel — anonymous conversion tracking to measure ad performance. No personally identifiable information is shared

Cookies and Tracking

This site may use cookies from Google Analytics and Meta Pixel for anonymous analytics and conversion tracking. These cookies do not identify you personally. We do not use cookies for advertising retargeting beyond basic conversion measurement.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone. Ever. Your data is shared only with the third-party services listed above, solely to deliver the services you requested.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Unsubscribe from our emails at any time using the link in every email we send
  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data from our systems

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@enterliminalspace.com.

Data Retention

We retain your email and name for as long as you remain subscribed to our list. If you unsubscribe, your data is removed from our active mailing list. You may request full deletion at any time.

Children's Privacy

Our site and products are not intended for anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, reach out to us at hello@enterliminalspace.com.
